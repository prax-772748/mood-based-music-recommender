Music Recommendation App - Quick Start Guide

To Start the App:
1. Double-click the "Music Recommender" shortcut on your desktop
2. Wait for the app to start (it will open in your web browser)
3. Enter your username to begin
4. Follow the on-screen instructions

Note: The first time you run the app, it may take a few minutes to load all required components.

If you need help:
- Make sure you have internet connection
- Try refreshing the page if it's stuck
- Close and restart the app if needed

Enjoy your personalized music recommendations!
